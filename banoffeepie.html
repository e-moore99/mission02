Banoffee Pie

The recipe below is my mum's recipe for Banoffee Pie. I think this is probably one of the first things I learned how to bake

Ingredients:

For the base:

  • 75g butter, melted (use more if necessary).
  • 1 pack of Superwine biscuits,

For the filling and topping:

  • 250g tub of cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1 tin of highlander caramel
  • 330ml bottle of cream
  • 1 flake
  • 2-3 bananas
  • Optional: 1 tbsp icing sugar

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C .
  2. Crush your Superwine biscuits using a rolling pin or food processer until coarse-fine crumbs.
  3. Mix melted butter with the biscuit crumbs until it has the consistency of wet sand, more or less butter may be needed.
  4. Press crumbs into your pie dish with an even thickness around the sides and bottom, bake for 15-20 mins or until lightly golden. Allow to cool.
  5. Mix together the cream cheese and caramel until they are homogenous. Spread over the pie base and put in the fridge until ready to serve.
  6. Just before serving, slice up your bananas and whip the cream, if you prefer sweetened cream add the icing sugar here.
  7. Place the bananas in an even layer on top the caramel, top with the whipped cream.
  8. Crumble the flake bar on top, cut, and enjoy!

Notes:

  • If the cream cheese is not room temperature it will be chunky and your filling will not be smooth
  • How many bananas you need really depends on the size of them, use your best judgement.
