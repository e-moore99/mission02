Flan

The recipe below is my partner's mum's recipe for Flan, this Argentinian dessert is one of my favourites.

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tins of condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 condensed milk tins full of milk
  • 1/4 cup white sugar

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Put the sugar into your flan tin and heat up on the stove, melting the sugar and cooking until caramelised. Tilt to coat the caramel up the sides of your baking tin.
  3. Using an electric mixer, beat the eggs until well combined.
  4. Add the condensed milk and mix to combine.
  5. Add the milk and vanilla, and beat for 5 minutes on a medium speed.
  6. Pour into the prepared flan tin and cover with tinfoil. Place inside a hot water bath and bake for 45-60 mins.
  7. When a knife is inserted and comes out clean, it is ready. Allow to cool fully and refrigerate before serving.

Notes:

  • Making a half recipe for a smaller dish works great for this recipe, just start checking if it's done at 45 mins.
  • If you like to have a lot of caramel on top, just use extra sugar.
