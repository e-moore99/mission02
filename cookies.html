Chocolate Chunk Cookies

The recipe below is for my reliable, go-to chocolate chunk cookies. These cookies are always a hit with my friends and family!

Ingredients:

  • 225g of butter, melted
  • 415g plain flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 320g brown sugar
  • 100g white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 340g chocolate, I prefer whittakers 50% dark block

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C and cut the chocolate into good sized chunks.
  2. Mix together the melted butter and sugars until smooth.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, ensuring the eggs are fully combined, then add the vanilla and mix well.
  4. Add all the dry ingredients, mix until just combined.
  5. Add chocolate and mix until combined. Allow dough to chill for at least 1 hour, up to overnight.
  6. Make ping-pong ball sized balls of dough, bake for 10-12 mins or until golden.

Notes:

  • You can make the cookies whatever size you like, just keep an eye on them and adjust cooking time accordingly.
  • I find this recipe is best when using a bar chocolate that is cut up, but you can use any kind of chocolate.
